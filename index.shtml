Welcome to Seshadripuram Independent Pu College

Seshadripuram Independent PU College is a powerhouse of knowledge. Here students are taken through a rigorous training to become empowered and responsible citizens. Best facilities and faculty ensure that all who pass through this hallowed portal are blessed with wisdom, knowledge and right values.

Dr. S. Radhakrishnan says "The end-product of education should be a free creative man, who can battle against historical circumstances and adversities of nature". We at SIPUC aim to prepare students to face the world with courage and confidence.
